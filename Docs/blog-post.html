From Lead to Enrollment: 10 Fixes That Cut CPA in Debt Funnels

Playbook • 7 min read

Debt funnels fail in predictable places: consent capture, dialer handoff, and offer clarity. Here are ten quick wins we deploy in week one.

  1. Require consent with versioned copy; log IP/UA/URL.
  2. Preserve UTMs end-to-end; avoid source overwrite on edit.
  3. Webhook retries with backoff and alerting.
  4. Dispositions mapped to lifecycle; no free-form labels.
  5. QA scorecards with weekly calibration.
  6. DTI/Hardship snapshot saved to the record.
  7. Offer comparator with disclosures, not only “savings”.
  8. Chargeback prevention via confirmation SMS/email.
  9. TV boards for transfer→show→close progression.
  10. Suppression lists applied in real time.
