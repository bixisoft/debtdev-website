    Frequently Asked Questions

    Get answers to common questions about our products, services, and implementation process.

    How long does implementation typically take?

    Implementation time varies based on the complexity of your requirements, but most clients are up and running within 2-4 weeks. Our streamlined onboarding process and pre-built solutions ensure a quick deployment with minimal disruption to your operations.

    Do you offer custom development for unique requirements?

    Yes, we specialize in custom development to address unique business requirements. Our team works closely with you to understand your specific needs and build tailored solutions that integrate seamlessly with your existing systems and workflows.

    What kind of support do you provide after implementation?

    We provide comprehensive ongoing support including technical assistance, regular updates, and proactive monitoring. All plans include email support, with priority phone support available for Professional and Enterprise customers. We also offer training resources and documentation.

    How do you ensure compliance with changing regulations?

    Our compliance toolkit is designed to adapt to regulatory changes. We monitor TCPA, DNC, and other relevant regulations continuously and push updates to all clients automatically. You also receive audit trails and reporting to demonstrate compliance during examinations.
